SWOT Analysis Training Game

How to Play

  1. Read each statement carefully
  2. Decide if it represents a Strength, Weakness, Opportunity, or Threat
  3. Click the corresponding button to answer
  4. Learn from the feedback and continue to the next statement
  5. Answer within the time limit to score points
  6. Build streaks and earn badges as you play!

Understanding SWOT Analysis

Strengths

Internal factors that give your organization an advantage.

Example: Strong brand recognition, skilled workforce

Weaknesses

Internal factors that place your organization at a disadvantage.

Example: Outdated technology, high turnover

Opportunities

External factors that could be beneficial to your organization.

Example: Emerging markets, new technologies

Threats

External factors that could be harmful to your organization.

Example: New competitors, changing regulations
